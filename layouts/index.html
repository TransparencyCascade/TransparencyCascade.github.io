Transparency Cascade Press is an independent investigative journalism operation founded by Mark Ramm, a writer and researcher with 20+ years of publication experience including a book with Prentice Hall and articles for O'Reilly Media.

We adhere to the Society of Professional Journalists Code of Ethics and publish our editorial policies publicly. Our methodology prioritizes documentary evidence, multiple independent sources, and transparent corrections.

Transparency Cascade Press, LLC is a Michigan limited liability company with no external investors and no hidden stakeholders.
